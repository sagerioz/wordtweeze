Welcome

  • Word Tweeze

    • Groom Your Essays, Letters and Poems with Tools that Build Language Skills

    Here you can find an easy to use text editor and word search feature that returns definitions, etymology, examples of use, related words and audio pronounciations. This provides you the opportunity to edit your work and choose different and better words in the writing process!

    As you learn to use the search function to investigate words, explore your results further by double clicking on any text on the page to automatically select new words to search.

    To get started, return to the home page and enter a word to tweeze. Or begin using the text editor to write something great.
